Our mission is to entertain gamers to keep them engaged with the experiences we provide. Our storylines are crafted with love and care. We want you to have fun and stay engaged.

About Us

Flywheel Entertainment started as a small company in St. Louis producing indie games. We started at only a couple developers and over time, we kept growing.

Supported Features

Flywheel Entertainment currently supports Playstation, Xbox, Nintendo, and PC. All of our games have cross-progression so you can save data between platforms. Nvidia RTX ray tracing is supported throughout all of our games.
